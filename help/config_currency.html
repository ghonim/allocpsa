Main Currency

This is the default currency as used in alloc. To modify the list that appears in the dropdown you may want to go to Setup - Miscellaneous - Currency Types and make certain currencies active or inactive.

If you are updating from a previous version of alloc that did not have support for currencies, you may want to select your preferred currency from the dropdown and click the "Update Transactions That Have No Currency" button.

Th old-style monetary transactions (that don't have a currency at all), will then be stored as the selected currency.
