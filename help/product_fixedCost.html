Product Costs

Enter any costs associated with the product. For example, the cost of acquiring the product, the postage and handling, and fixed commissions etc.

The Sell Price minus the sum of all the fixed Product Costs, will be the margin/profit from the sale.

For example: If the Sell Price is $100 and you have two product costs: $60 to acquire the item, and $10 postage. Then the margin/profit will be $30.

The margin/profit will be later distributed based on the Product Commissions percentages that you set up below.
