Effort Limited To

The number of hours entered here gives an indication of the maximum amount of hours that are to be time sheeted for this task.

Should the amount of hours billed exceed this figure, a warning will appear on the related time sheet.

When the task is first being created, the value entered for the Most Likely will be copied into this field.

If there's still no value in the Effort Limited To, then the Default Task Limit field on the Task's Project, will be used.
