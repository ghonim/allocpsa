Time Sheet Help

This page will let you create a Time Sheet and submit it to a Project Manager or Time Sheet Admin.

1. Select the Project that this Time Sheet is for.

2. Click the Create Time Sheet button.

3. A new box will appear at the bottom, this will enable you to associate Time Sheet Line Items to this Time Sheet. Enter the Date, Duration, Amount, select the Task that you are billing for, and click the Add Time Sheet Item button. A new line entry will appear below.

4. Continue filling out line entries for this Time Sheet until you have reached a satisfactory total, or you have reached the amount that the invoice will be for, for this Time Sheet.

5. When you are done (over a period of days, weeks etc) click the To Manager/Admin button. An email will be sent off to the Manager or Admin for this Time Sheet.

6. Additionally you can click a printout link in the top right hand corner to open a new browser window with a printer friendly version of the Time Sheet that can be printed out and eg: sent for invoicing.
