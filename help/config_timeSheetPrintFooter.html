Time Sheet Printout Footer

When generating a PDF version of a Time Sheet it may be desireable to have a customized footer. This footer could contain your organizations bank details etc. Basic html such as <br>, <b> and <u> are allowed and will be rendered as expected in the PDF document (br means new line, b means bold, u means underline. The b and u tags must encapsulate their content with an ending tag eg: </b>).
