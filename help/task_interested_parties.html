Interested Parties
This will let you nominate other people who may be interested in receiving email about this Task, by selecting one or more names from this list.

The people selected as Interested Parties will have their names automatically selected when adding Task Comments.

Be careful who you put as an Interested Party, because most likely they will see most email about this Task.

The list of people is made up from the Client Contacts of the Project that this Task belongs to, as well as the people that are assigned to this Tasks Project. Users or Clients without an email address will not be displayed in this list.

In addition this list will have any Default Interested Parties as specified on the main allocPSA Setup page.
